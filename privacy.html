TypingTales Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Summary

TypingTales collects no personal information. Period.

Data Collection

TypingTales does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All game progress is stored locally on your device using standard browser/app storage and never leaves your device.

No Analytics

We do not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

No Third-Party Services

The only network communication is with Apple's App Store servers for in-app purchase verification. No other third-party services are used.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

TypingTales is designed for children ages 6-10. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or anyone else. There are no accounts, no social features, no ads, and no data sharing.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email privacy@typingtales.com
